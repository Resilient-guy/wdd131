Nigeria

Nigeria, officially known as the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country located in West Africa. It covers an area of 923,768 square kilometers and has a population of approximately 236 million people, making it the most populous country in Africa and the sixth most populous in the world. The country is bordered by Niger to the north, Chad to the northeast, Cameroon to the east, and Benin to the west. The capital city is Abuja, located in the Federal Capital Territory, while Lagos is the largest city and the commercial and industrial hub of the country.
